You can follow these steps to check:

1. On your keyboard, press**Win+I** (the Windows logo key and the i key) at the same time to open the Windows Settings app. Click**System** .

5. Click **Start test** and tap or speak to your microphone. Then click **Stop test** . If you‚Äôre prompted **The highest value we saw was xx (xx>0) percent** , it means your microphone is working correctly.
